) camp side.

- Address: Ashbourne Road, Hurdlow, Derbyshire, England, SK17 9QQ
- Phone: +44 1298 83718

We are going to book 2 Bell tents for two nights 15-17 Sept. TBC.

At the moment we have booked one Bell tent for the first night.
